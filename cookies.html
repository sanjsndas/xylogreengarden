Manage Your Cookie Preferences

How to manage:
  • Browser settings: Most browsers accept cookies by default, but you can modify settings to block or delete cookies (Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies; Safari: Preferences → Privacy).
  • Opt-out of Analytics: You can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Our Consent Tool: On your first visit, we display a cookie banner where you can accept, reject, or customize non-essential cookies. You can revisit the banner by clicking the "Cookie Settings" floating icon (bottom-left) or by contacting us.

For any questions regarding cookies or to withdraw consent, email privacy@xylogreengarden.com.

We honor Do Not Track (DNT) signals where technically feasible, and we are committed to transparency. Our cookie inventory is reviewed quarterly.

GDPR & CCPA Rights

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you have specific data protection rights. Xylo Green Garden is committed to honoring these rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell personal data, but you can still contact us to confirm.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Information: We never collect sensitive data without explicit consent.

To exercise these rights, please email privacy@xylogreengarden.com. We will respond within 30 days. California residents may also have the right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights. We also provide a "Shine the Light" disclosure upon request.
