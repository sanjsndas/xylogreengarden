What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help recognize your preferences and improve functionality.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for site navigation, security, and core features (e.g., form submissions).
  • Analytics/Performance Cookies: We use Google Analytics to understand visitor behavior and improve content. These cookies collect anonymized data.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as language or region.
  • Marketing Cookies: Limited to future remarketing campaigns (only with explicit opt-in).
